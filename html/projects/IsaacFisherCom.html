    It's my personal website. isaac-fisher.com will be the home of everything I'll write, build, draw, cook, or photograph for the foreseeable future. I'll be keeping a technical blog to discuss some of the projects I've been working on lately, and a photos page to post some of the food I've cooked or pictures I've taken.
