BookBroker

Spring 2025 - https://github.com/agiledev-students-spring2025/4-final-bookbroker

BookBroker [external link] is an app designed to streamline the process of trading used books and encourage readers to discover new material in the most sustainable, cost-effective, community-driven way possible: peer to peer. Our mission is to enable everyone to read - it's one giant collective library.

BookBroker's tech stack is a university classic: MongoDB, Express, and React.js, but we opted for Tailwind to streamline styling as well. It's hosted on DigitalOcean. As a final project for my Agile Development class, a core part of the development experience was adherence to the Scrum method.
