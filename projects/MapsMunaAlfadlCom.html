Maps by Muna al Fadl

Fall 2025 - https://maps.munaalfadl.com

This web app [external link] is a collaboration with Syrian-American artist Muna al Fadl to showcase her process in her Google Earth series of paintings. I've previously worked with Muna to build her personal website. The maps project is a public-facing site and an admin dashboard to allow Muna to curate and build the piece as she expands her Google Earth collection.

I built the app with Next.js and Tailwind CSS, along with the open-source mapping library Leaflet. Authentication, file storage, and database are handled by Supabase. The app is deployed via Vercel and is intended to be a standalone piece alongside Muna's personal site.
